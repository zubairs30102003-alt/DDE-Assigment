# WHU Otto Beisheim School of Management - Brand Library

This comprehensive brand library contains all official WHU branding specifications for consistent application across artifacts, documents, and presentations.

## Color Palette

### Primary Brand Colors

#### WHU Blue (Signature Color)
- **Hex**: `#2C4592`
- **RGB**: `rgb(44, 69, 146)`
- **Usage**: Primary headers, titles, key accents, navigation elements, bullet points
- **Applications**: Section headers, call-to-actions, emphasis elements

#### White
- **Hex**: `#FFFFFF`
- **RGB**: `rgb(255, 255, 255)`
- **Usage**: Primary backgrounds, text on dark backgrounds, clean spaces
- **Applications**: Page backgrounds, cards, contrast elements

#### Black
- **Hex**: `#000000`
- **RGB**: `rgb(0, 0, 0)`
- **Usage**: Primary body text, strong emphasis
- **Applications**: Paragraph text, headings on light backgrounds

### Secondary Brand Colors

#### Light Blue
- **Hex**: `#808FBE`
- **RGB**: `rgb(128, 143, 190)`
- **Usage**: Secondary accents, hover states, softer highlights
- **Applications**: Secondary buttons, subtle highlights, backgrounds

#### Dark Gray (Text Secondary)
- **Hex**: `#515256`
- **RGB**: `rgb(81, 82, 86)`
- **Usage**: Secondary text, captions, metadata
- **Applications**: Subtitles, descriptions, less prominent text

#### Medium Gray
- **Hex**: `#A29795`
- **RGB**: `rgb(162, 151, 149)`
- **Usage**: Tertiary elements, subtle dividers
- **Applications**: Borders, separators, inactive states

#### Light Gray
- **Hex**: `#C7C1BF`
- **RGB**: `rgb(199, 193, 191)`
- **Usage**: Background accents, card backgrounds
- **Applications**: Subtle backgrounds, hover effects

#### Very Light Gray
- **Hex**: `#EEEBEA`
- **RGB**: `rgb(238, 235, 234)`
- **Usage**: Subtle backgrounds, section dividers
- **Applications**: Alternating row backgrounds, page sections

### Accent Colors

#### Red (Links & Alerts)
- **Hex**: `#E7331A`
- **RGB**: `rgb(231, 51, 26)`
- **Usage**: Hyperlinks, important callouts, warnings
- **Applications**: Links, important notices, error states

## Typography

### Font Families

#### Primary Font: Arial
- **Headings**: Arial, Bold
- **Body Text**: Arial, Regular
- **Captions**: Arial, Regular or Italic
- **Fallback**: Helvetica, sans-serif

### Font Sizes and Hierarchy

#### For Web/HTML Artifacts
```css
/* Headings */
h1: 36px (2.25rem) - Bold - #2C4592
h2: 28px (1.75rem) - Bold - #2C4592  
h3: 24px (1.5rem) - Bold - #2C4592
h4: 20px (1.25rem) - Bold - #515256
h5: 18px (1.125rem) - Bold - #515256
h6: 16px (1rem) - Bold - #515256

/* Body Text */
Body: 16px (1rem) - Regular - #000000
Large Body: 18px (1.125rem) - Regular - #000000
Small Text: 14px (0.875rem) - Regular - #515256
Caption: 12px (0.75rem) - Regular - #515256
```

#### For Documents (Word, PDF)
- **Title**: 36-44pt, Arial Bold, WHU Blue (#2C4592)
- **Heading 1**: 28-32pt, Arial Bold, WHU Blue
- **Heading 2**: 24pt, Arial Bold, WHU Blue
- **Heading 3**: 20pt, Arial Bold, Dark Gray
- **Body Text**: 11-12pt, Arial Regular, Black
- **Captions**: 9-10pt, Arial Regular, Dark Gray

#### For Presentations (PowerPoint)
- **Slide Title**: 28-36pt, Arial Bold, White (on blue) or WHU Blue (on white)
- **Body Text**: 16-20pt, Arial Regular, Black
- **Bullet Points**: 16-18pt, Arial Regular, Black
- **Captions**: 12-14pt, Arial Regular, Dark Gray

### Line Height
- **Headings**: 1.2-1.3 line height
- **Body Text**: 1.5-1.6 line height
- **Captions**: 1.4 line height

## Logo Specifications

### WHU Logo Usage

#### Logo Placement Guidelines
- **Presentations**: Top right corner on content slides, bottom right on title slides
- **Documents**: Header (top right) or footer area
- **Web/HTML**: Top navigation bar or footer

#### Logo Specifications
- **Format**: SVG preferred, PNG with transparent background acceptable
- **Minimum Size**: 120px width (digital), 1 inch width (print)
- **Clear Space**: Minimum padding of logo height × 0.5 on all sides
- **Background**: White box/background recommended for visibility

#### Logo Variations
- **Primary**: Full color WHU logo on white
- **Reversed**: White logo for dark backgrounds
- **Monochrome**: WHU Blue logo for single-color applications

## Layout Patterns

### Slide Layouts (PowerPoint)

#### Title Slide
- Large branded header bar in WHU Blue (#2C4592)
- Title text: 36-44pt, Arial Bold, White
- Subtitle/Author: 18-24pt, Arial Regular, White
- WHU logo in white on blue background (top or bottom right)
- Optional: Full-width background image (campus/professional imagery)

#### Standard Content Slide
- **Header Bar**: 
  - Full-width blue (#2C4592) bar at top (height: ~1 inch)
  - Slide title: 28-32pt, Arial Bold, White
  - WHU logo in white box at top right
  
- **Content Area**:
  - White background
  - Left-aligned content with generous margins
  - Bullet points with WHU Blue color
  
- **Footer** (optional):
  - Light gray bar (#EEEBEA)
  - Page numbers, date, section name: 10pt, Dark Gray

#### Section Divider Slide
- Full-width WHU Blue background
- Section name: 36-44pt, Arial Bold, White
- Optional: Icon or minimal graphic element
- WHU logo in white

### Document Layouts (Word, PDF)

#### Header
- **Height**: ~0.75 inches
- **Content**: Document title or section name (12pt, Dark Gray)
- **Logo**: WHU logo in top right (0.75-1 inch width)
- **Border**: Optional thin line in Light Gray (#C7C1BF)

#### Body Layout
- **Margins**: 1 inch all sides (or 2.5cm)
- **Columns**: Single column for reports, optional 2-column for newsletters
- **Spacing**: Double-spaced between sections, 1.5 line-height for body text
- **Alignment**: Left-aligned text (not justified)

#### Footer
- **Height**: ~0.5 inches
- **Content**: Page number, date, department name (10pt, Dark Gray)
- **Border**: Optional thin line in Light Gray

### Web/HTML Artifacts

#### Navigation Bar
- **Background**: WHU Blue (#2C4592)
- **Height**: 60-70px

  - Background: WHU Blue (#2C4592)
  - Text: White, Arial Bold, 16px

## Bullet Point Specifications

### Standard Bullet Points
- **Character**: § (Section symbol, Unicode U+00A7)
- **Font**: Wingdings character § OR Unicode section symbol
- **Color**: WHU Blue (#2C4592)
- **Size**: Match text size
- **Indent**: 0.25-0.5 inches from margin
- **Hanging Indent**: 0.25 inches

### Alternative Bullet Styles
- **Level 1**: § in WHU Blue
- **Level 2**: ○ (hollow circle) in Light Blue
- **Level 3**: – (en dash) in Dark Gray

## Image Guidelines

### Image Specifications
- **Quality**: High resolution (minimum 1920px width for full-width images)
- **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency, SVG for icons
- **Aspect Ratios**: 16:9 for slides, 3:2 or 4:3 for documents
- **Subject Matter**: Campus imagery, professional business settings, academic environments

### Image Treatment
- **Overlays**: When placing text over images, use semi-transparent WHU Blue overlay (rgba(44, 69, 146, 0.85))
- **Borders**: Optional 1px border in Light Gray for contained images
- **Spacing**: Minimum 20px margin around images in documents

## Design Principles

### Professional Academic Aesthetic
- **Clean**: Generous white space, uncluttered layouts
- **Structured**: Clear visual hierarchy with consistent spacing
- **Trustworthy**: Professional color palette, readable typography
- **Accessible**: High contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for body text)

### Brand Consistency Rules
1. **Always** use WHU Blue (#2C4592) for primary branding elements
2. **Never** substitute Arial with decorative or script fonts
3. **Maintain** logo clear space and minimum size requirements
4. **Preserve** visual hierarchy with proper heading levels
5. **Apply** consistent spacing and margins across all materials

## Accessibility Guidelines

### Color Contrast
- **Text on White**: Use Black (#000000) or WHU Blue (#2C4592)
- **Text on WHU Blue**: Use White (#FFFFFF) only
- **Text on Light Backgrounds**: Minimum contrast ratio 4.5:1
- **Large Text** (18pt+): Minimum contrast ratio 3:1

### Alt Text
- Provide descriptive alt text for all images
- Include WHU logo alt text: "WHU Otto Beisheim School of Management"
